Bed & Breakfasts


Warwick Valley B&B

Walk or drive: 0.8 miles away;
4 min. drive or nice 17 min. walk through the village center.

Owner, Loretta.
If you call, tell her you're visiting Valerie Lacey. I've been in here. Lovely. I've had a friend stay and she loved it.

Sleepy Valley Inn

Not walkable. 2.6 miles away; 8 min. drive.

Owners, John & Peggy.
If you call, tell them you're visiting Valerie Lacey. I've never been in here. Can't attest, but John and Peggy are friends, and also own Yesterday's Restaurant in the village.


Chateau Hathorn

3 minute drive

I've never been in the guest rooms, but have dined here. Excellent food.

AirBnB & Vrbo


Search Warwick, NY

Zoom the map in to Welling Avenue, Warwick, NY, to see how close any listing is to the house.

Here are just a few that are in walking distance:

"The Warwick View - Top Floor Flat" looks pretty nice and is walking distance to 23 Welling Ave.

"Warwick Village Deli House Down" is within walking distance from Welling Ave. Appears on the map to be right around the corner.

"Warwick Getaway in the Heart of the Village" is also within walking distance of Welling Ave.

"The Cottage at Millpond Falls" looks fabulous and you'll likely pass it on your way in. Very short drive to the house.

Hotels


Holiday Inn, Chester, NY

10 miles, 20 min drive

Hotel, motel, Holiday Inn!



Note: I do not recommend Warwick Motel & Suites!

Transportation


Taxi or Group Arrangement

There are several local taxi services that can be called.
Or, we can make a group arrangement with a local service, depending on where everyone winds up staying.
Uber & Lyft are tough to get around here.
